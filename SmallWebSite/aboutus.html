About us

Slammers Beach Volleyball is a local volleyball club, that puts most of its emphasis on training players and developing their volleyball skills and passion.
There are around 100 players playing at Slammers each week, we believe in the good sportsmanship and discipline molding youth into successful adults.

This year, we are looking to have both professional and regular teams.
for regular teams, we have completed facilities to develop player's interests.
Moreover, the professional level teams will look to play in competitive games and have opportunities to played in the professional volleyball league,
our coaches will build great systems around the athlets, remain objective and provide a high ceiling of development.
